Frank Eluaka

Overview

I'm a Business Data Analyst based in the vibrant city of Toronto, my passion for data analytics is palpable. When I'm not immersed in the world of data, I often find myself at the park, basking in the beauty of nature or spending quality time with my family. This deep-rooted passion for data analytics has led me to constantly seek new ways to learn and expand my knowledge, making it a space I've truly fallen in love with!

The narratives that numbers weave have always captivated me. My journey began with an analytical foundation in the concepts of econometrics and my early career roles, where I honed a strong knack for strategic thinking, problem-solving, and identifying patterns in complex data.

My previous roles in customer-centric positions have taught me how to approach challenges from a human-centred perspective, and my and my passion for analysis has driven me to uncover insights that genuinely make a difference. Currently, I am concentrating on obtaining relevant certifications in Business Intelligence, Analytics, and Business Analysis to enhance my technical skills further and delve deeper into the continuously evolving realm of data.

Throughout my academic and professional journey, I've had the opportunity to work on exciting projects where I analyzed large datasets, created data pipelines, and delivered compelling insights. I've gained valuable experience with tools like Python, SQL, and PowerBI and hands-on experience in data management and statistical analysis. Whether building interactive dashboards or uncovering trends buried deep within data, I thrive on finding creative solutions that drive tangible business outcomes and deliver value to stakeholders.

The future of data analytics is a thrilling prospect that I eagerly anticipate. Whether I'm collaborating with a team or working solo, my drive for excellence and the boundless potential of data continue to fuel my passion!
